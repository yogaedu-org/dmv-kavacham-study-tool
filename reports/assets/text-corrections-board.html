Current
…āgneyāṃ ṣoḍaśī pātu…
Proposed
…āgneyyāṃ ṣoḍaśī pātu…

Why: the Devanāgarī already reads आग्नेय्यां with a doubled य्य. The IAST dropped one y — it should be āgneyyāṃ (locative of āgneyī). Only the romanization changes.
