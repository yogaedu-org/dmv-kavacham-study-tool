## Daśamahāvidyā Kavacam

### Verse 1 (anuṣṭubh, ~8+8+8+8)
Sanskrit: oṃ prācyāṃ rakṣatu me tārā / kāmarūpanivāsinī / āgneyyāṃ ṣoḍaśī pātu / yāmyāṃ dhūmāvatī svayam

Singable English (candidate):
> In the east, may Tārā keep me,
> she of Kāmarūpa's dwelling;
> southeast, Ṣoḍaśī will guard me,
> in the south, Dhūmāvatī reigns.

Notes: each line ≈ 8 syllables to sit on the chant's phrases. "reigns" carries *svayam*
(herself). Adjust to the real note count when the tune lands.
